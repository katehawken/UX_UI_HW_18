About me and my journey into
the UX / UI world

Past experience includes Configuration Assistance and Project Administration in both the Recruitment and Construction industry in Australia and New Zealand.

UX / UI Design has since become a passion.

It’s all about problem solving and understanding why humans do the things they do. A curious mind, always needing to understand why things are the way they are. Additionally the minimalistic and clean UI that goes hand in hand with UX is amazing.

Conducting in-depth user research and brainstorming solutions, as well as the process of using design thinking to build a concept and then converting it into a usable product and the bonus of the UI elements just makes that even better.

Collaboration and teamwork is fun!
